  • Two ships, Sagar Tara and Sagar Anveshika have been constructed and are taking up coastal research activities.

  • Established Hyperbaric Test facility for 900 bar (First in South East Asia) and Underwater Vehicle Test Pond facility in NIOT Campus

  • As part of collaborative project with Anna University, set up a deep sea vehicle testing facility with simulated soil conditions

  • The state of the art submersibles and integration facility and Buoy test facility have been established at NIOT.

  • Acquired land to set up world class sea front facility for testing of underwater vehicles, desalination, underwater acoustics etc.

  • Hydro-transport test facility established at Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Madras for Polymetallic Nodule Mining Research with active participation of NIOT team.

  • Vessel management Cell: Four ships of NIOT starting with two coastal research vessels, buoy tender vessel and a world class technology demonstration vessel (dynamically positioned) are managed successfully.

  • Actively involved in setting up of Fluid Control Research Institute, set up under assistance from United Nations Development Programme (UNDP) Project, in the initial phase of career.
